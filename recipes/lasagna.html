Lasagna Recipe

A piece of lasagna

  • 1 lb ground beef
  • 1 jar marinara sauce
  • 9 lasagna noodles
  • 2 cups ricotta cheese
  • 2 cups shredded mozarella cheese
  • 1.2 cup grated Parmesan cheese
  • 1 egg
  • salt and pepper to taste
  1. Cook the meat
    • In a large skillet, cook the ground beef over medium heat until browned. Drain any excess fat. Add the marinara sauce and simmer for 5 minutes.
  2. Prepare the cheese mixture
    • In a bowl, mix together the ricotta cheese, egg, salt, and pepper.
  3. Cook the noodles
    • Cook the lasagna noodles according to the package instructions. Drain and set aside.
  4. Assemble the lasagna
    • Preheat your oven to 375°F (190°C).
    • Spread a thin layer of meat sauce in the bottom of a 9x13 inch baking dish.
    • Place 3 noodles over the sauce. Spread 1/3 of the ricotta mixture over the noodles. Sprinkle with 1/3 of the mozzarella cheese. Repeat layers twice more, ending with a layer of meat sauce and a final sprinkling of mozzarella and Parmesan cheese.
  5. Bake
    • Cover with aluminum foil and bake for 25 minutes. Remove the foil and bake for an additional 25 minutes. Let it cool for 10 minutes before serving.
